Lumo StoryTime

Your Data & Privacy

Our goal is to design Lumo StoryTime to protect your data and privacy while still making it fun and useful.

No Account Required

Lumo StoryTime doesn't require an account to use it:

  • Your name or email are not required to make stories, audiobooks, or heroes.
  • Your stories, heroes, and settings are saved on your device until you turn on Lumo Cloud.

How Personal Information is Used

When you use Lumo StoryTime, we only use what's needed to make the app work:

  • The hero you picked or Magic Camera picture you took to create your story.
  • The story you wanted to turn into an audiobook.
  • The reader's age, reading level, and language.
  • Your name, if you turned on "Include Me in the Story."
  • Details you enter to create a custom hero, like appearance, skills, and description.

This information is first sent securely to Lumo's servers so we can process your request, protect the service, and send it to the right tools.

We then share the minimum needed with the Artificial Intelligence (AI) models we use to create stories, audiobooks, and heroes. These may be provided by companies like OpenAI, xAI, and ElevenLabs.

App Improvement

We collect anonymous details on how you use Lumo StoryTime to make it better for you and others.

This can include things like:

  • How often the app is used.
  • Which features are used.
  • Whether story creation, reading, purchases, and cloud features are working well.
  • General details like age band, story language, or story type.

We use Firebase Analytics from Google for this.

We collect this information when you install the app, but you can turn it off in Settings.

We use a unique but anonymous ID to understand how people use the app over time.

  • This anonymous ID is reset once per year.
  • If you turn off "Share How You Use StoryTime" and back on again, you will have a new anonymous ID.

How We Make Money

Lumo StoryTime makes money when you buy in-app purchases of Stars or subscribe to Star Pass.

We don't show ads or sell your data to make money.

Stars

Stars are how you pay for things in Lumo StoryTime.

  • You are given free Stars to get started.
  • You can buy more Stars with real money using In-App Purchase.
  • Stars are saved to an anonymous account tied to your app.
  • You will likely lose your Stars if you delete or reset the app.
  • Your Stars can be saved to a Lumo account if you create one or sign in.

Lumo Cloud

Lumo Cloud is included with Star Pass:

  • It keeps a backup of your stories, heroes, Magic Camera photos, settings and more.
  • If you turn on Lumo Cloud on your other devices, it will also keep your Lumo StoryTime in sync between them.

Lumo Cloud requires a free Lumo account so we know who your data belongs to.

If you turn off Lumo Cloud, StoryTime stops backing up and syncing going forward. Data already saved in Lumo Cloud stays there until you delete your Lumo account and cloud data.

Magic Camera

Magic Camera lets you create stories from a photo.

When you use Magic Camera:

  • The photo you take is sent to the AI model to inspire the details of your story.
  • Those photos are saved in the story they helped create; they are not added to your photo library.
  • You can delete a photo anytime by deleting the story.

You can turn off Magic Camera in settings if you prefer not to use it.

How Your Data is Saved

Lumo StoryTime keeps your data in two ways:

  • Your stories, heroes, Magic Camera photos, settings and more are saved on your device.
  • If you use Lumo Cloud, your data is also saved to your Lumo account.

Data saved to Lumo Cloud is encrypted in transit and at rest.

You Are In Control

You have control over your data:

  • Turn off "Share How You Use StoryTime" in Settings.
  • Delete stories and their photos any time.
  • Reset All Stories and Settings to start over.
  • Turn off "Include Me in the Story" in Settings.
  • Turn off Lumo Cloud to stop future backups or syncing.
  • If you're signed in, delete your Lumo account and its cloud data from the reset flow.

We also use a stable device ID stored on your device to help protect your account, stars, and cloud features. We limit the information we share with AI companies to the minimum needed to create your stories, heroes, and audiobooks.

Policy Updates

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll notify you in the app. By continuing to use Lumo StoryTime, you agree to the latest version of this policy.
