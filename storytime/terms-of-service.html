How Your Data is Used

Our aim is to maximize your family's privacy whenever possible.

We use artificial intelligence (AI) to create your stories, audiobooks, and heroes using models provided by companies like OpenAI, xAI, and ElevenLabs.

When you use these features, the app securely sends your request to Lumo's servers first. From there, we send the minimum needed to the AI tools that create your story, audiobook, or hero. That request can include things like:

  • The hero you picked or photo you took to create a story
  • The story you're turning into an audiobook
  • The reader's age, reading level, and language
  • Your name and gender, if you turn on "Include Me in the Story"
  • Details you enter when creating a custom hero, like appearance, skills, and description

Lumo also collects anonymous usage information through Firebase Analytics to help us understand how often the app is used, which features are used most, and whether things are working well.

  • "Help Us Improve StoryTime" is on by default
  • You can turn off this setting at any time
  • We use a unique but anonymous ID to understand how people use the app over time
  • This anonymous ID is reset once per year

We never use your data for ads, and we never sell it to anyone.

Magic Camera

Magic Camera lets you take a photo and use it to inspire a story. When you use it:

  • The photo is sent to an AI model to understand its contents and use it as the basis of the story
  • The photo is saved alongside the story it helped create
  • It is not added to your photo library
  • If you turn on Lumo Cloud, stories and their photos are uploaded to your Lumo account
  • To delete a photo and its story, tap and hold the story, then choose "Delete Story"

You can turn off Magic Camera at any time in the app's settings.

Please only upload photos you own or have permission to use. Avoid uploading anything copyrighted, inappropriate, or harmful.

Accounts, Backups, and Resetting

You can use Lumo StoryTime without creating an account. By default, your stories, heroes, and settings are stored only on your device.

If you subscribe to Star Pass, you can create a free Lumo account using Sign in with Apple and enable Lumo Cloud. Lumo Cloud saves a backup of your stories, heroes, photos, and settings and lets you sync them to your other devices.

Resetting:

  • "Reset All Stories & Settings" deletes your on-device library and resets the app
  • Turning off Lumo Cloud stops future backups and syncing, but does not automatically delete data already saved in Lumo Cloud
  • If you're signed in, you can also choose to delete your Lumo account and your data in Lumo Cloud

Stars are tied to your app and can also be saved to your Lumo account when you create one or sign in. Deleting or resetting the app may result in losing your Stars.
